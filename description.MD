# Company Description

Dharma Datatech is a company based in Uberlândia, MG, Brazil, focused in the development of advanced Analytics, Machine Learning and AI-driven systems, especially for **consuming goods industry**.

Dharma's founder, Lucas Rodrigues, phD, is an Engineer with over 15 years of experience with research & development, automation & robotics, and DataOps specifically for insdustrial operations. The company now is a resident service provider for the top players in consuming goods around the world.

# Scope of projects

Dharma works with projects of:
1. Real-time Machine Learning predictions to improve quality, performance and safety of industrial processes;
2. Soft-sensing and Digital Twin Operations;
3. Secure integration between Factory Floor networks (PLC, IoT Mesh, etc) with Cloud-based systems;
4. Enterprise AI solutions integrating multi-level information systems with LLMs using MCP;
5. Predictive maintenance using AI-powered models;
6. Integration of Agentic AI on Business Intelligence systems;
7. Process optmiziation in production processes and logistic operations;
8. Carbon emission reduction solutions following the GHG Protocol (Scope 1 to 3).
